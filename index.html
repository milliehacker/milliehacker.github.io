I'm a 1st-year linguistics PhD student at Michigan State University and RA for the mi-diaries.org. I am interested in all things phonological variation, and compelled by the central question of how (un)markedness interfaces with the core grammar.

I'm currently investigating how vernacular features are brought out by narrative speech and fast speech in the MI Diaries corpus. My goal is to help distinguish the contributions of overlapping contextual factors in the emergence of the unmarked.
