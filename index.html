I'm a 1st-year linguistics PhD student at Michigan State University and an RA for the MI Diaries corpus project. I am interested in all things phonological variation, and compelled by the central question of how (un)markedness interfaces with the core grammar.

I'm currently investigating the ways in which non-standard features are brought out by narrative speech and fast speech. My goal is to help distinguish the contributions of overlapping contextual factors in the emergence of the unmarked. The variable I'm focusing on right now is the (infamous) word-final ɪŋ/ɪn in English.
